# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want Parallax to be a respectful, rigorous, and welcoming project. Contributors should feel safe asking questions, proposing ideas, and offering feedback without being dismissed or demeaned.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent while staying honest
- Critique ideas and implementation choices, not people
- Help keep technical discussions clear and grounded
- Accept feedback with curiosity

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Intimidation or sustained disruption
- Public or private behavior that makes participation feel unsafe

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code of conduct, contact the maintainers through the repository support channels described in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md).
